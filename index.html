Intro

Ever since I was little, I have always been fascinated by technology. How robots think, communicate between eachother, and how we interact with them seemed so cool to me! Diving right into it though can be extremely daunting, so I had only ever scraped the surface of how to build websites in highschool. While on the topic, check out my awesome work!

With this fascination of technology always present, it was fairly difficult to solely choose as a career path since my main goal in life is to help people and I did not know how that was possible... until now!
